FAQ

How much does a bot cost?

Depends on the task: lead bots — from $500, payments & subscriptions — from $900, mini apps — from $1,500. You get the exact price after we discuss the task — before work starts, and it doesn't change.

How long does a typical bot take?

A lead-capture bot — from a few days to a couple of weeks. Projects with payments and AI — usually 2–4 weeks. The timeline is locked in the proposal along with the price.

What do you need from me as a client?

Describe the task in your own words and stay reachable for questions — no formal spec required. Accounts (payment provider, hosting) are registered in your name; I'll help set everything up.

What happens after launch?

30 days of free fixes after delivery. Then — support by agreement, or I hand the project over entirely: everything runs on your side, all access is yours.

How does payment work?

Officially: sole proprietor, contract, bank transfer. Milestone payments — part upfront, the rest on delivery of the working bot.

Who will build my project?

Daniil personally runs every project — development and all communication. Design and testing are handled by the team.

Do you build turnkey?

Yes: logic, payments, server, launch. You get a working product — not code you still have to figure out where to put.
