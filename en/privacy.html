Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 12, 2026

What we collect

Only what you type into the site's forms (Contact and Become a partner): a way to reach you (Telegram handle or email), your project description or a few words about yourself and, optionally, a budget range.

For traffic analytics the site uses Yandex Metrica (Yandex LLC). It sets cookies and collects anonymised technical data: IP address, device and browser type, referrer, pages viewed and actions on them. This data does not identify you personally, is processed on servers in the Russian Federation, and is used only to understand how to improve the site. No third-party ad trackers are used. You can opt out by blocking cookies in your browser settings or installing an extension that blocks analytics counters.

How it’s used

Solely to reply to your enquiry: discuss the task, prepare a quote and, if we agree to work together, deliver the project. Your details are never used for mailing lists, never sold and never shared with third parties for marketing.

How it’s delivered

Your enquiry is first written to Yandex Object Storage in Russia (region ru-central1). The primary record of your data is stored in the Russian Federation. After that we receive a notification of the enquiry as a Telegram message (Telegram Messenger Inc.). No copy is stored on this website. It is a static site with no database.

Retention and your rights

We keep your enquiry only as long as needed to handle it. You can ask what we hold about you, have it corrected or deleted at any time. Just message us via the contact page.

Changes

The current version of this policy is always published on this page; the date above changes when the policy does. The Russian version of this site is additionally governed by a policy under Russian data-protection law (152-FZ).
